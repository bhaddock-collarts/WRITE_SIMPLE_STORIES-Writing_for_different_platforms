Learning Objective

By completing this activity, you will be able to identify and distinguish between different storytelling mediums and their key characteristics, supporting your understanding of how to write simple stories for various platforms.

How to Play

  • Step 1: Click or tap on a Medium card (left side) to select it
  • Step 2: Then click or tap on a matching Characteristic card (right side)
  • Step 3: If they match, both cards will turn green
  • Step 4: If they don't match, they'll turn red briefly - try again
  • Step 5: Match all 7 pairs to complete the activity
  • Tip: Use the Hint button if you need help with a match
